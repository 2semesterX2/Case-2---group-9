To begin the game you just have to enter your name on the main screen and click " Start Quiz!"

After the game has begun you will receive 10 questions, one by one.

Read the questions carefully and the 4 possible answers. When you are ready click on the answer you believe is correct.

If you were right your score will be updated and you will proceed to the next question. Do the same for each of the questions.

Please note that each question has only one correct answer.

At the end of the game your final score will be displayed.
